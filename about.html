About.

Bringing Nature Home – Minus the Fuss

We’re a bunch of creatives, plant nerds, designers, and décor buffs who all want the same thing: to bring the chill vibes and beauty of nature indoors, without the hassle of keeping real plants alive.

It all started because we saw folks needed plants that were easy to care for, looked good, and lasted. So, we asked ourselves:

Could we get that relaxed feeling you get from real plants, but without the need for sunlight, water, or, you know, actually caring for them?

Now, we’ve got a bunch of awesome fake plants that look super real. We’re talking about plants with textures, colors, and little quirks that make them look like the real deal. Even people who know their plants might do a double take!

Our fake plants aren't just copies – they're like nature, but leveled up with cool designs and ways of making them that last long. We wanted plants that were easy and would last for a long while.

🧭 Our Deal

To make homes, stores, parties, and everywhere else look great with fake plants that are beautiful and don’t need any taking care of. It’s all about bringing nature to everyone, no matter where they are or what their life is like.

🌍 What We’re Aiming For

We want everyone to be able to have plants around them that make them feel good. If you live in a tiny apartment, work in a dark office, or have a patio that gets wrecked by the weather, fake plants are a fix that looks good, lasts, and makes things better.

We want to be known worldwide for making the best-looking fake plants that are good for the planet and that people love.
